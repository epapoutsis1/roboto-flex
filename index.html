History of the Font

Roboto Flex has 12 fonts. It was originally designed for the operating system. Its grade, weight, width, and optical sizes allow flexibility to be used across a variety of platforms. This is similar to Google's flagship brand, Roboto typeface. However, Roboto Flex takes adaptability a step further by enabling designers to fine-tune its appearance with greater precision. This dynamic style makes it widely recognized and ideal for designers while it maintains readability across different screen sizes and resolutions.
